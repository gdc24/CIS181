Players' Theatre Group at CWRU

History

Since its inagural season in Spring 2005, Players' Theatre Group has always been a force for student theatre at Case Western Reservce University. Our plays are all student produced, directed, designed, and acted. Check out our past shows page to see past productions, or click here to get involved!

Mission Statement

Players' Theatre Group (PTG) is a performance group which allows undergraduates the unique experience of taking charge of all aspects of theatrical performances. The shows we perform are student proposed, directed, designed and performed. Our current home is the Eldred Black Box Theater.

Exec

As a USG organiztion, our exec board consists of a president, vice president, treasurer, and secretary. In 2016, the technical director position was added to better suit the needs of our club. Our exec boards are elected at the end of November and serve for a calendar year. Interested in running? Check out our members page and get in contact with the current position holder, or email playerstheatregroup@gmail.com for more info!
